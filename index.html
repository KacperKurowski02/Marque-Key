How it works

Five phases.
Zero compromises.

The process is designed so that your team performs at their natural, everyday level — not a rehearsed version of it. That is the only way the findings are worth acting on.

01
Contract & deposit

Agreement signed, 50% deposit received. Audit window set — the GM knows an audit is coming, not when.

02
Pre-arrival tests

Online booking UX, telephone reservation, and email enquiry evaluated before any physical visit.

03
Anonymous visit

Auditor stays as an ordinary paying guest. All 12 touchpoints assessed in real time. Identity never disclosed.

04
Report delivery

Scored PDF report delivered within 7–10 business days. Balance invoice issued simultaneously.

05
GM debrief

60-minute video session with the General Manager. Findings presented, questions answered, priorities set.

The auditor's identity is never disclosed — not during, not after.

Staff will not know an audit is taking place. The exact date, room booking, and auditor alias remain confidential at all times. This is non-negotiable — and it is what makes the findings credible.
